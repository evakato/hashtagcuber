Cubing Resources

My bookmarks bar was too disorganized, so I made this instead.


2x2

  • Best 2x2 Algs: Just like the title says. A list of the best CLL, EG1, EG2, LEG-1, TCLL, and more algs documented by the best 2x2ers in the world.
  • My EG2 Doc: Eva Kato's personal list of EG2 algs, many of which are also anti-CLLs.

3x3

  • All of Jabari's Algs: Pretty much everything Jabari has genned in a nice folder.
  • My OLLCP Doc: Eva Kato's personal list of OLLCP algs, but I only put the ones I thought were worth learning.
  • Cale's OLLCP Doc: Probably the most up to date collection of OLLCP algs, nice alternatives are also provided. (Thanks Cale!)
  • Optimal ZBLS: Algs to orient edges for each F2L case, provided by CubeZone.
  • ZBLL Trainer: Awesome ZBLL trainer designed by Roman.
  • Jabari's ZBLL: One of the most popular lists of ZBLL.
  • Rob Yau's ZBLL: I'm not actually sure if Yau was the creator of this list, but many algs are influenced by him. This doc has a lot of not well-known but great algs and alternatives.
  • Eva's 1LLL: My small list of 1LLL found by hand.

4x4

  • Easy OLL + Parity Cases: Feliks's setup moves before OLL parity to skip OLL.

5x5-7x7

  • L2E Algs for Big Cubes: Feliks's algs for parity last two edges, considered the best list among many.

OH

  • CPEOLL: Antoine's algs to solve corners and EO to set up a 2GLL.

3BLD

  • Speedsolving's Letter Pairs: Thorough list of letter pairs words with many alternatives.
  • Josh Weimer's UBL: His reduced 3style is very useful.
  • Ishaan's UBL and DF: Probably the best and most developed set of 3style algs for these buffers.
  • BLD Algs Collection: Roman's website with all the good BLDer's alg docs.

Megaminx

  • Megaminx PLL: James Macdiarmid's complete list of megaminx pll, organized extremely well.

Square-1

  • Rowe and Tommy's CSP: Most popular CSP document with provided reference schemes for BLD tracing.
  • Squanmate: Super awesome collection of tools, including a CSP trainer and EP trainer.
  • Intuitive PLL: Carter's list of square-1 PLL with solely combos of CP and/or PBLs with small move counts.

4BLD

  • Noah's Beginner Tutorial: Noah Arthurs's Beginner 4BLD tutorial + algs.

Other

  • CYO's Resources: An original list of all the key documented algs online, however, a bit outdated.
  • Alg Sorter: Simple tool to rewrite algs cleanly.
  • WCA Ranking Sorter: Program that sorts your WCA results based on world ranking, created by Brandon Lin.
